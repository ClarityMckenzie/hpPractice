Curry Udon Recipe


  • Ingredients
  • onion
  • garlic
  • potato
  • carrot
  • stock
  • sake
  • Glico curry
  • beef
  • udon
  • Directions
  • Saute onion, garlic, potato, carrot.
  • Add stock, sake, and Glico curry.
  • Sear beef in skillet.
  • Combine with boiled udon noodles.
  • Garnish.
